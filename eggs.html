Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is a scrambled eggs recipe with tomatoes! It is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tomato
  • teaspoon of oil

Steps

  1. Add the eggs into a bowl and mix with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the pan and add oil
  3. Add diced tomatoes to the pan
  4. Stir the tomatoes and then add the eggs
  5. let it cook and enjoy!
