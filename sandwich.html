Sandwich

Description

This is a simple sandwhich recipe, which is easy to make and tasty!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • potato
  • tomato
  • butter
  • onion
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the onions, potatoes, and tomatoes and add them to the bread
  2. heat the pan and add a bit of butter to the bread and the pan
  3. season the inside of the sandwich
  4. lay on the pan
  5. flip
  6. Enjoy!
